Particle Swarm Optimization

  • Optimization algorithm
  • Random initial particles and speed
  • Update:
    v_i(t+1) = w * v_i(t) + c_1 * rand * (pbest_i - x_i(t)) + c_2 * rand * (Gbest - x_i(t))
    x_i(t+1) = x_i(t) + v_i(t+1)
